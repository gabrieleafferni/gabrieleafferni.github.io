Projects

01

HMM–LSTM hybrid for probabilistic forecasting

MSc thesis · paper in preparation

A hybrid model combining Hidden Markov Models (to capture latent market regimes) with LSTM networks, for probabilistic forecasting of S&P 500 returns over 1/3/6-month horizons. Full Python pipeline: data preparation, walk-forward validation, multi-seed ensembling, and a battery of formal statistical tests. It systematically beats the reference statistical benchmarks.
